About Helmet and Number Plate Detector

This application utilizes state-of-the-art object detection techniques to detect and identify helmets and number plates in images or videos. It employs the YOLO (You Only Look Once) model for accurate and efficient detection. Whether you need to monitor safety compliance in industrial settings or analyze traffic violations, this detector offers reliable results.
